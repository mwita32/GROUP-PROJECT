User Guide

1. Applicant Login

The first window you will see is the Applicant Login. If you don’t have an account, click the Register button to create one.

2. Registration

Fill in all the fields correctly. Use a valid email address because you will receive an activation link via email. Click that link to activate your account before logging in.

3. Login and Dashboard

After activation, log in using your credentials. You will be redirected to the Student Dashboard.

4. Bursary Application

To apply for the Rattansi Bursary, click on Application under your dashboard. Make sure you read the Instructions carefully before proceeding.

The application form has 4 sections:

  • Section 1: Personal Details
  • Section 2: Family Background
  • Section 3: Other Information
  • Section 4: Declaration

Complete all fields, attach necessary documents, and click Submit Application. You'll receive a confirmation message upon successful submission.

5. Notifications

Track your application status under the Notification section on the dashboard.

6. Inquiries

For any assistance, use the Contact Us section under the dashboard.

7. Allocation

Allocation will occur immediately after the application deadline.The application form will be disabled after the deadline, and you won’t see the Application option.
